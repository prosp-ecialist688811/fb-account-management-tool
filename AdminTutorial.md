## Implementation of Technical Features

### Facebook Account Warm-Up

The **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature serves as the cornerstone of effective account management. This functionality helps automate the process of nurturing accounts, simulating behaviors typical of active users such as browsing homepage feeds, liking posts, and commenting on videos.

#### Key Features:

- **Behavior Simulation**: The tool intelligently navigates between homepage feeds and video pages, ensuring that it emulates real user behavior effectively.
  
- **Interaction Settings**: Users can configure specific parameters such as interaction probability and execution distribution, tailoring the warm-up process to meet their unique account needs.

#### Technical Steps to Configure:

1. **Access the Tool**: Open the fb-account-management-tool application on your preferred platform (real Android device, cloud phone, or emulator).
   
2. **Navigate to Warm-Up Settings**: Locate the **Facebook Account Warm-Up** section in the interface.

3. **Set Interaction Probability**: Adjust the interaction probability settings to control how often the tool engages with different types of content (e.g., likes vs. comments).

4. **Execution Probability Distribution**: Configure the execution probability to allocate different weights to various interactions, ensuring a balanced and organic-looking engagement pattern.

5. **Start the Warm-Up**: Activate the warm-up process, and the tool will begin simulating user interactions continuously.
